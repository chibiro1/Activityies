Chicken Salad

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens(lettuce, spinach, etc)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Prepare the Vegetables:
    - Wash the chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and bell pepper.
  • Mix the Salad:
    - In a large bowl, combine the chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and mixed greens.
  • Make the Dressing:
    - In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  • Toss the Salad:
    - Pour the dressing over the salad.
    - Toss gently to coat everything.
  • Serve:
    - Serve Immediately.
